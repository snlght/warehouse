ERP EXO APPLICATION

EXO

SYNOPSIS

EXO (EXOSCULAT, Latin for calculation) is an automated system for tracking provided services, their tariffication, and billing. EXO is a universal manager of client accounts containing the history of tariffed transactions. Accounts are controlled by BPMN processes whose activities are defined by Erlang functions. EXO, as an example of ERP.UNO, can be used as a prototype for building billing systems, banks, and other accounting systems.
